Villa 111 in Lavasa

Villa 111

Nestled in the heart of scenic Lavasa, this contemporary styled villa offers you tranquility in the lap of luxury. Step onto the the pool deck and take a plunge in our pool while enjoying the scenic forest and mountain views around. Enjoy a drink with your group at the open air bar and covered gazebo by the pool or spend the afternoon playing with children in the vast and lush outdoor areas of the villa.

This 4 bedroom villa is spread across a 1 acre plot and features spacious rooms to keep you and your loved ones comfortable. Another interesting feature in this property is the treehouse in the backyard where you will truly feel nested away deep in the forest with exotic birds all around.

Spread across a 1 acre plot, this stunning home features spacious rooms to keep you and your loved ones comfortable and ample outdoor areas.

What we find most interesting about this property, is that in the backyard, a few feet beyond the villa, there was a steep drop. This cliff has been painstakingly filled with 100s of trucks worth of soil, and very lovingly nurtured and designed. Around 800 trees have been planted in the property and you will truly feel that you are nested away deep in the forest with exotic birds all around.

The villa features elegant interiors and a vast living and dining area. There is a modern, custom-made deck accessible from the living room.

The villa is built on two levels. The upper level houses the living are, 6- seater dining area, two bedrooms with ensuite bathrooms, a powder room and a custom-made deck. The lower level has an indoor bar, lounge area with books and games, two bedrooms, and steps out into the backyard.

DETAILS:

  • The Villa 111 accomodates 8 (+4) guests.
  • 4 spacious bedrooms with ensuite bathrooms.
  • Large Sub-tropical garden with Gazebo and a Powder Room
  • Large living room with dining area and outdoor deck
  • Carrom board, board games and books
  • Modern tree house and indoor and outdoor bar
  • Wardrobes and luggage racks are available in all rooms so feel free to unpack on your arrival.
  • Infinity Swimming Pool with adjoining deck and loungers.
