Villa 106 in Lavasa

We offer 4 luxurious bedrooms in this well furnished and immaculately maintained villa. We have options of additional villas in close proximity to accommodate larger groups.

STEPPING IN

We have recently added this villa to our collection for those looking for a more economical stay but with the Soul Tree Villa standards of service and exclusivity. This 4 bedroom villa was is ideal for people looking to escape the city and spend time with nature, while still enjoying the comforts of home. The villa features beautifully landscaped gardens all around, an open air bar under a covered gazebo, outdoor seating and barbeque arrangements. With windows on all sides, sunshine pours into every corner of the villa and you will surely feel a sense of rejuvenation after your stay here.
