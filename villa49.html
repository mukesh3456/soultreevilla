Villa 49, Lavasa

Villa 49 is located on a quaint, scenic street with expansive views of the lake, dam and mountains. This four bedroom villa, built over 3 levels, offers tranquility and luxury. With windows on all sides, sunshine pours into every corner. The living and dining areas, with their double ceiling height, have a very majestic feel. Have your morning cuppa at our English styled bar, which also doubles as a work station for some.

The four bedrooms are spacious and all open onto balcony with breath-taking views. You can step into a garden for some outdoor fun or just curl up and read a book from our library. If that's not enough, step out on the large wooden deck overlooking the lake, where you can enjoy a special meal, a barbeque, or just a drink.

About Soultree Villas

We built our dream holiday home in Lavasa in December, 2019. The idea was to have a haven to enjoy the company of friends and loved ones. Then in March, 2020 the first lockdown was announced, and we as a family spent a few months in Lavasa. That's when we truly fell in love with Lavasa, and decided to open up our home for others to enjoy.

The aim of Soul Tree Villas is to provide luxurious living in the non-commercial locales of Lavasa, to give our patrons a truly relaxing time away from the hustle bustle called city life! We now have options of villas for you to choose from, whether you are looking for the lake waters to calm you, or the serenity of the forest to make you feel closer to nature.

Renting a villa from us not only gives you access to a stylishly accentuated home, but also a well-trained team of chefs and attendants. All you have to do is showup, and take in the breathtaking views. We take care of the rest.

The Master Bedroom has high ceilings and french windows which open into a wrap around balcony, overlooking the lake and the sprawling hills. After a morning walk, indulge in a luxurious salt bath in the bathtub to awaken your senses.

There is one bedroom located on the top level with an adjoining home gym, activity area with board games and outdoor patio. The other two bedrooms are located on the bottom level and open out into a huge wooden deck with spectacular views.

Step into nature from within your villa, onto any one of our many outdoor spaces. Choose from two wooden decks overlooking the lake, a garden, a patio overlooking the hills or any one of balconies adjoining the bedrooms.

A glimpse of the airy and sunlight drenched living and dining areas with floor to ceiling windows. The wooden bar, with its rustic high table and bar chairs, is the perfect set-up for bottomless brunches.
